The Urban Water Hackathon

The Urban Water Hackathon is presented in conjunction with the NYC Water Summit. This is the first year of the hackathon, and the theme is exploring urban water data. Several cities from around the world have donated anonymized data for you to hack on.

Create new apps, maps, insights, and connect people to their water in amazing new ways.

Urban Hackathon Rules

Prizes and Judging

Prizes will be awarded for the three best projects. There will be three judges who will review each project for code quality, relevance to the water community, and potential impact for a city. Judges have not yet been selected for the event.
